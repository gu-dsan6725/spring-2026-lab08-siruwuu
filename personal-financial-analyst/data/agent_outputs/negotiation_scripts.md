## Priority 1: Comcast Internet — $89.99/month → Target $59.99/month

**Savings: $30/month ($360/year) | Success Probability: 75%**
**Best Time to Call:** Tuesday–Thursday, 10 AM–2 PM | **Call:** 1-800-934-6489

### Script
> "Hi, I'm calling because my promotional rate has expired and my bill is now $89.99/month. I've been a loyal customer and I've been seeing AT&T Fiber offering service for $55–$65/month. I'd like to explore how to stay with Comcast at a competitive rate, otherwise I'll need to consider switching."

**Key Leverage:** Competitor quotes (AT&T Fiber, Spectrum, T-Mobile Home Internet at $50/mo)

**Objection Handling:**
- *"That's our standard rate."* → "I understand, but I've been a loyal customer. I've seen promotional rates of $55 online. Can you apply a loyalty discount or match a promo rate?"
- *"I can add a channel bundle."* → "I'm looking for a rate reduction, not additional services. Can you connect me to your retention department?"
- *"Best I can do is $79.99."* → "I appreciate it, but it's still higher than competitors. Let me verify AT&T availability and call you back."

**Escalation:** Ask for supervisor → ask for "loyalty/retention department" — they have more authority.

**Best Time to Call:** Tuesday–Thursday, 9 AM–3 PM

### Script
> "Hi, I'm reviewing my auto insurance premium. I'm currently paying $185/month and I've been a customer with a clean driving record. I've received quotes from Geico and Progressive at $115–$130/month for similar coverage. What options do we have to keep my rates competitive?"

**Key Questions to Ask:**
1. What discounts am I currently receiving?
2. Am I eligible for loyalty discounts (3+ years as customer)?
3. What if I increase my deductible from $500 to $1,000?
4. Would bundling home/renters insurance give me a multi-policy discount?
5. Do you offer a "Drive Safe & Save" telematics discount?
6. Can I save by paying annually instead of monthly?

**Target:** $135/month | **Stretch:** $120/month with bundling

**If Negotiation Fails:** Get written quotes from Geico, Progressive, and Allstate (20 min online), then call back and say: "I've decided to switch to [Geico] at $118/month. Is there anything you can do to keep me?"

## General Negotiation Tips

1. **Always have competitor quotes open** before calling — it's your strongest leverage
2. **Ask for the retention/loyalty department** — they have authority to offer deals reps can't
3. **Best days to call:** Tuesday–Thursday | **Best time:** 10 AM–2 PM
4. **Get confirmation in writing** — ask for email summary with new rate and terms
5. **Be polite but firm** — threatening to cancel is your most powerful tool; only use if you mean it
6. **Call back if rejected** — different representative may offer a better deal
7. **Document everything** — note rep name, reference number, date, and promised rate
